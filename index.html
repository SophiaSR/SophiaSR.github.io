Sophia Roshal

I am a third year PhD student in computer science at CSD @ Carnegie Mellon University, advised by Frank Pfenning, working towards efficient and usable functional programming languages. Current work has been on an adjoint natural deduction system with the goal of compilation down to the semi axiomatic sequent calculus (which has a futures model of parallelism interpretation). Previously, I was an undergraduate at Cornell University, where I worked under Dexter Kozen and Pedro H. Azevedo de Amorim on categorical semantics for mixing parallel and sequential operations. Pedro created this website template.

I am primarily interested in programming language design.
